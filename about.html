Our Story

Learn how we're revolutionizing grocery shopping in Abuja

From Idea to Reality

Stretch Buy was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make grocery shopping in Abuja more convenient, efficient, and stress-free. Our founders recognized the challenges that residents faced when trying to access quality groceries from their favorite supermarkets amidst busy schedules and traffic congestion.

What started as a small operation connecting a few supermarkets in Wuse and Maitama has grown into Abuja's leading grocery delivery service, partnering with over 20 supermarkets across the city and serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services to meet the growing needs of Abuja residents while maintaining our commitment to quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

To revolutionize grocery shopping in Abuja by providing fast, reliable, and convenient delivery services that save our customers time and effort while supporting local supermarkets.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted grocery delivery platform, expanding to major cities while maintaining our commitment to quality service and community support.
