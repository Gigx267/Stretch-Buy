Our Story

Learn how we're transforming grocery shopping in Abuja

Our Beginning

Stretch Buy was founded with a simple mission: to make grocery shopping convenient for busy Abuja residents to serve thousands of customers across the city.

Our Growth

we've expanded our network to include supermarkets across Abuja. Our customer base has grown as more people discover the convenience of our service.

Our Community

We're proud to be part of the Abuja community, creating jobs for delivery personnel and helping local supermarkets reach more customers.

Our Mission

To revolutionize grocery shopping in Abuja by providing fast, reliable, and convenient delivery services that save our customers time and effort while supporting local businesses.

Our Vision

To become Abuja's leading grocery delivery platform, known for exceptional service, wide selection, and commitment to customer satisfaction, while expanding to serve more cities across Nigeria.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Speed & Efficiency

We understand that time is precious. That's why we've optimized our processes to ensure your groceries arrive as quickly as possible.

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We continuously improve our service based on your feedback and needs.

Support Local

We're committed to supporting Abuja's local economy by partnering with supermarkets and creating employment opportunities.

Quality Assurance

We maintain high standards for all our partner supermarkets and delivery personnel to ensure you receive the best service possible.
